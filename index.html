My love,

I don’t know how to begin this without my heart sinking. I know I’ve hurt you — not because I stopped loving you, but because my mind has been a storm I couldn’t silence. The truth is, I’ve been battling things I didn’t know how to explain. And instead of reaching for you, I pulled away. I thought leaving would spare you from carrying the weight of me... but now I see that in trying not to be a burden, I became one in the worst way.


Please understand — I didn’t walk away because I stopped loving you. I left because I didn’t feel enough, because I was scared I’d drag you into the mess I was stuck in. But that was wrong. I see that now. Love isn’t about disappearing when things get dark — it’s about learning to walk through that darkness together.


And I still love you. So much. More than I’ve ever been able to say with words. That love never left — it’s still here, quietly beating under everything, asking for a second chance.


I know “sorry” isn’t enough. I know I have to show you. And I will.


I’m not promising perfection, but I am promising effort. Real, consistent effort to heal, to grow, and to be the kind of man who shows up — especially when it’s hard. I’m learning, slowly, how to face my demons instead of running from them... and from you.

I want to win you back, not with grand gestures, but with daily proof that I mean what I say — that I’m better this time, and I’m choosing to be better every day that follows.


If your heart still has space for me — even a little — I’ll wait. I’ll fight. I’ll find a way back to you, step by step, with honesty, patience, and love.


You don’t owe me anything, not even another chance. But I owe you everything — including this truth: I love you. I never stopped. And I’m still here.


Always

Zild
